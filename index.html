Characters in Noli Me Tangere

Juan Crisóstomo Ibarra y Magsalin A young mestizo who has just returned to the Philippines after almost a decade of studying in Europe. A very outgoing person with a charming personality. He is a very idealistic person just like his father.
Don Rafael Ibarra He is Crisóstomo's deceased father. He died in prison before he could clear his name under charges like heresy.
Lieutenant Guevara He is the lieutenant of the Civil Guard. He fully supports Ibarra and the deceased Don Rafael, and is very vocal about his opinions regarding Father Dámaso.
Father Sibyla One of the many priests in the story, Father Sibyla is a calm and logical person. He serves in the Binondo district in the city of Manila. He is the exact opposite of the corrupt Father Dámaso and the difficult Father Salví.
Father Salví Another one of the priests in the story, Father Salví is a more cunning and antagonistic character that strategizes well and uses his religious role as a priest for his personal agenda and desires.
